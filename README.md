# HealthNexus Medical Records Platform

HealthNexus is an advanced AI-powered health records management system that bridges the gap between doctors and patients.

## ğŸš€ Key Features

### For Patients
- **AI Report Analysis**: Upload lab reports (PDF/Image) and get instant AI-generated summaries and trend analysis.
- **Family Health**: Manage health records for your entire family in one place.
- **Smart Connectivity**: Link with your doctor via QR code for seamless record sharing.
- **Medicine Explainer**: Get AI-powered video explanations for prescribed medicines.

### For Doctors
- **Patient Dashboard**: View a consolidated history of all your patients.
- **Prescription Pad**: Digital prescription writing with AI assistance.
- **Secure Access**: Access patient records securely with permission-based sharing.
